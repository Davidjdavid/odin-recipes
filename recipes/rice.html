Rice Recipe

This dish is a white rice meal. You must have a rice cooker for this meal to work. There are only 2 ingredients in this meal. Very easy to make and tastes great with any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry white rice
  • Water

Steps

  • Gather the ingredients.
  • Wash rice in a strainer until starch is no longer visibly washing off
  • Put washed rice in rice cooker and fill to appropriate water line
  • Close rice cooker and run the machine. Wait until rice is done.
  • Serve and enjoy!
